The Wind Rises


The Wind Rises is a 2013 Japanese animated historical drama film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki, which was animated and published by Ghibli Studio. The story follows and is inspired by the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the main subject of the film.

  • Naoko Satomi
